AI Noise Reducer: Powerful AI Denoising for Clean Audio

AI Noise Reducer is an iOS audio denoising app built for creators, musicians, students, and anyone who wants clearer sound. Import audio or video in seconds, remove noise with advanced AI processing, and export clean, high-quality results.

Key Features:

1. Advanced AI Noise Reduction

Use powerful AI denoising technology to suppress background noise, hiss, hum, and environmental interference while preserving voices and important sound details.

2. Flexible File Import

Import audio and video through multiple methods:

  • Files: Import directly from local files.
  • Photos Videos: Import media from your album.
  • Recordings Music: Bring in recordings and music tracks.
  • Wi-Fi Transfer: Transfer files wirelessly from computer.
  • AirDrop: Quick sharing between Apple devices.

3. Broad Format Support

Supported import formats include:

mp3, wav, flac, m4a, ogg, aac, wma, aiff, alac, amr, ac3, opus, caf, dts, wv, oga, w64, voc, spx, ircam, sf, mp2, gsm.

4. High-Quality Export

After denoising, export clear audio suitable for podcasts, interviews, voice notes, short videos, online classes, and more.

  • Fast Processing: Optimized performance for quick results.
